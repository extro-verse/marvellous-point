Steps to Access Files on Telegram

Step 1: Select the Image Banner

Choose the image banner representing the file you wish to access on the app.

Step 2: Click the Image

Tap or click on the image to open the file details.

Step 3: Scroll Down to File Information

Scroll down through the file details until you see the "Access Files" box.

Step 4: Join and Download

First, press the "Access" button to join the area where the file is located. Then, click the "Download" button to retrieve the file.
